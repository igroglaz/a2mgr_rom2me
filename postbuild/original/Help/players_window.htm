Players Window

In this section you can change player's attributes.

When you make your selection, please press the Add player button. If you wish to cancel the selection - press Delete player button.

Also, in this menu you can select the Diplomacy relations between selected and other players. Diplomacy determines how the other player relate to your character. You can choose from selection box:

Enemy - When this option is selected - all other players will immediately attack you, when you reach their location.

Neutral - When this option is selected - all other players will not attack you, but you could attack them.

Alliance - When this option is selected - all other players will never attack you & you can not attack them either. You can attack your allies only if you turn on the force mode.

Vision - When this option is selected - player will share your unit's field of vision. 

Please note while changing your current character - the maximum number of players is limited to 16. Also, never use the same color for two different players.
